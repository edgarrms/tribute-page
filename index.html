According to wikipedia José Álvaro Osorio Balvín is a Colombian reggaeton singer. He has won five Billboard Latin Music Awards, four Latin Grammy Awards, two MTV Video Music Awards and four Latin American Music Awards and received two Grammy Award nominations.
J Balvin as he is better known on stage, is an inspioration to all latinos. He represents one of the best performers of the latin community. Balvin has been called the "Drake" of the latin world. Born in Medillin Balvin, shows love and respect to the latin community from his top notch music to his loving inspirstional words towards helping others.
